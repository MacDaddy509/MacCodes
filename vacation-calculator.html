Balance Before Vacation (C10, C11, C12):

=B3+(B4*ROUNDDOWN((A10-TODAY())/14,0))

Balance After Vacation (D10, D11, D12):

Running Total (B14):

=B3+SUM(B10:B12)+(B4*ROUNDDOWN((MAX(A10:A12)-TODAY())/14,0))

  5. Add the projected balance formula in B6

  7. Add balance calculation formulas in C10:C12 and D10:D12
  8. Add the running total formula in B14

Note: These formulas assume bi-weekly pay periods (14 days) and calculate from TODAY(). Adjust the pay period interval if needed.
